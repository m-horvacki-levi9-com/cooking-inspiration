Japanese Chicken Curry

A gentle, savory curry with tender chicken, sweet carrots, and potatoes served over rice.

🍽 4 servings 🍛 Comfort classic
  • 1 lb chicken thighs
  • 2 potatoes
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 onion
  • 3 curry roux cubes
  • 3 cups cooked rice
  1. Brown the chicken and onion.
  2. Simmer with potatoes and carrots until tender.
  3. Melt in the curry roux and serve over rice.

Sheet Pan Sausage and Gnocchi

Crisp gnocchi, sweet peppers, and savory sausage all roasted on one tray for easy cleanup.

🍽 4 servings 🔥 One-pan dinner
  • 1 lb shelf-stable gnocchi
  • 12 oz Italian sausage
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 1 red onion
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  1. Toss gnocchi, sausage, and vegetables with oil and seasoning.
  2. Roast until everything is caramelized and crisp.
  3. Serve hot straight from the pan.

Lemon Herb Salmon Tray Bake

Fresh salmon, crisp asparagus, and fluffy couscous finished with a quick lemon dressing.

🍽 2 servings 🐟 Bright and fresh
  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 bunch asparagus
  • 1 cup couscous
  • 1 lemon
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp chopped dill
  1. Roast the salmon and asparagus until just cooked.
  2. Prepare couscous while the tray bake finishes.
  3. Toss everything with lemon juice and dill.

Brown Butter Mushroom Pasta

Deeply savory mushrooms, silky pasta, and nutty brown butter with a shower of parmesan.

🍽 4 servings 🍄 Rich umami
  • 12 oz pasta
  • 12 oz mixed mushrooms
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp fresh thyme
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan
  1. Brown the butter and sauté mushrooms until deeply golden.
  2. Add garlic, thyme, and cooked pasta with pasta water.
  3. Finish with parmesan for a glossy sauce.
